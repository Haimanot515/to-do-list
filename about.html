About To-Do Master

To-Do Master is designed to help you stay organized, manage your tasks efficiently, and improve your productivity. Our app allows you to add, edit, delete, sort, and filter tasks seamlessly.

We believe that organization is key to success. That's why we've built a platform that is simple, fast, and accessible from anywhere.

Features Overview

  • Add, Edit, Delete tasks
  • Sort tasks by due date
  • Filter tasks by category
  • Task Templates for fast organization
  • Team collaboration (coming soon)
